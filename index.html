Lingua.Live

Helping language learners grow through conversation with native speakers

PRODUCT

LinguaLive fosters students' practical application of language skills acquired in the classroom by providing a platform for learners to engage in structured conversations with native speakers. We collaborated with secondary language students and professional linguists on this project after observing that lanugage learners need a way to practice speaking because the goal of learning a new language is to have-real-world conversations.

THE SCOPE

  • Create web responsive platform for students to practice speaking the language they are learning with native speakers.
  • Enable students to engage in converstion topics of personal interest.
  • Give students a sense of progress throught structed dialogues that leverage knowledge acquired in the class classroom.
  • Allow native speakers to provide constructive feedback to foster student's development.

DESIGN PROCESS

Research

This process includes user interviews, competitive analysis, research, persona development, user stories and determination of the information architecture for the produc. Below is a quote frome one of our user interviews that encompasses a common sentiment of our users

"The goal of learning a new language is to be able to have real-world conversations." Yulia Russian Language Student

The problem

After interviewing several language students, we came up with a problem statement that encompasses the key pain point we aimed to solve with design thinking.

Language learners need a way to practice speaking because the goal of
learning a new language is to be ablt to have real-world conversations.

Cross platform experience

In order to make the on demand learning experience more accessible, we deemed web responsivity to be crucial to our users. This allows students to easily have conversations at any time or on the go.
